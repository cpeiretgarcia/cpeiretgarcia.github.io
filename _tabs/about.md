I am a **PhD researcher** at the [Centre for Doctoral Training (CDT) in Geospatial Systems](https://geospatialcdt.ac.uk/) at Newcastle University (UK). Prior to joining the CDT, I studied Economics and Regional and Local Economic Development at the University of Valladolid (Spain), and Geospatial Data Science at Newcastle University.

My research focuses in using geospatial data for better understanding people's mobility behaviours. I am particularly interested in how internal and external factors such as demographcis, the built environment, or accessibility might influence transport inequalities, and how the use of data can help identifying those populations most likely to suffer from these inequalities.
