Clara Peiret Garcia

I am a Research Fellow in Urban Analytics at the Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis at UCL.

I'm a researcher working at the intersection of spatial analytics and transport research, with a focus on travel behaviour and transport inequalities. My work explores how different people engage with the transport system, and how we can design more just transport policies.

From February 2026 I am a Smart Data Research UK Fellow. My project aims at generating mobility profiles at high spatial resolution. You can find out more about it here.
