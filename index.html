About

I am a Research Fellow in Urban Analytics at the Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis at UCL. From February 2026 I am a Smart Data Research UK Fellow. My project aims at generating mobility profiles at high spatial resolution. You can find out more about it here.

Before joining UCL, I completed my PhD in Geospatial Data Science at Newcastle University. My thesis looked at the development of travel profiles using survey data, and how these profiles can help us identify inequalities in mobility and accessibility across demographic groups. I also hold a Masters in Geospatial Engineering (Newcastle University) and a Masters in Regional and Local Economic Development (Universidad de Valladolid).

My Work

My work is at the intersection of spatial analytics and transport research, with focus on travel behaviour and transport inequalities. I am interested in how different people engage with the transport system, and what inequalities stem from these behaviours.
